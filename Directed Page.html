Who We Are

  Radical Energy & Materials (REM) is a technology and software company operating through the production and provision of products and services geared towards further commercializing the nuclear industry. REM currently operates in automatic cataloging and storage of nuclear waste to improve the use of nuclear medicine in hospitals. Our goal is to give hospitals the opportunity to provide better treatment and help the lives of more people.
