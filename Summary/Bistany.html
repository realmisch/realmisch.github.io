Sean Bistany

  Sean is a fourth-year undergraduate student majoring in Nuclear Engineering with a minor in Environmental Engineering. He is currently doing research in computational fluid dynamics focusing on gap instability between fuel rod bundles. He has been heavily involved with the Penn State Nuclear Engineering organizations, serving on the executive board of all four organizations. The Penn State American Nuclear Society chapter has elected to form a student conference committee to bid for the 2024 ANS student conference with Sean serving as the General Chair.
