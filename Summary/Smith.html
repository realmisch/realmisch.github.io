Coleman Smith

  Coleman Smith has served as the CEO of Radical Energy and Materials LLC since it was founded in 2021. Mr. Smith is a member of the American Nuclear Society and has presented award-winning work in radiation protection and shielding. In 2021, he received his Reactor Operator's License from the Nuclear Regulatory Commission at the Penn State Breazeale Reactor. Mr. Smith is a fourth year undergraduate student in Nuclear Engineering in the Schreyer Honors College at the Pennsylvania State University. He is also pursuing a minor in Environmental Engineering.
