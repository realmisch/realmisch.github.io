James Peretz

  As Chief Marketing Officer, Peretz is responsible for overseeing the planning, development, and execution of Radical Energy & Materials marketing and advertising initiatives to gain revenue and increase sales. Peretz is an upcoming Senior at Pennsylvania State University studying Nuclear Engineering. Previously, Peretz has interned in 2021 at Exelon Generation in Kennett Square, PA working on running MCNP 6.2 code to perform criticality safety benchmarking analysis on spent fuel pools. This upcoming summer of 2022, he will be working at the Limerick Generating Station in Pottstown, PA. He has also been very involved in many organizations during his time at Pennsylvania State University and is very interested in the rapidly growing future of nuclear energy as well as its applications.
